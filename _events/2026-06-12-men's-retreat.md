This InterPlay weekend for men is an opportunity to engage each other in deep
play and tap into the wealth of information we hold in our bodies.

<!--more-->

The incremental forms of InterPlay based in voice, movement, contact, and
stillness are easy-to-learn tools that enable us to discover new information
and weave stories from our daily lives. Join us to reaffirm your desires, your
dreams, and the satisfaction of living in a whole-bodied way Come to have ease,
fun and bump into wonder.
