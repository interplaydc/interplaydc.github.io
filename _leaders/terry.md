Terry McCarthy is a musician, teacher, performing artist with 20 years of 
experience teaching InterPlay.

<!--more-->

He is a founding member of Off the Deep End Ensemble and former director of 
Inner Voices vocal ensemble. He and his wife Laurece West led InterPlay 
performance troupe Impromptu Players. Laurece and Terry write and perform their 
original songs together. Terry is a former elementary school teacher with a BS 
degree in Arts for Children with Music Emphasis. He teaches InterPlay 
playgroups, performance, storytelling and writing classes. Terry has served as 
a bodyspirit mentor with the Raleigh InterPlay Life Practice Program for over 
6 years.
